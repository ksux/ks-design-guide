Language

Clarity and brevity

Strive to be concise with content, while using terminology that helps the user quickly understand the task at hand. Use bullet points as needed to highlight key information or steps.

Tone

Use friendly but professional sentences. Communication with students may be more informal, but avoid slang.

Punctuation

Use periods only after complete sentences, and be consistent in the meaning associated with recurring punctuation.
